ABOUT Convolu-ted

The Golden age of information is upon us and the yearn for authentic music is at an all time high. ConvoluTed, via his lyrics, originality, and songwriting process, takes pride in embodying and reflecting a journey of expansion. Going within and connecting with the infinite stream is the most fruitful and inspiring approach to creating in ConvoluTedâ€™s eyes. The greats have emulated, mastered, and passed down what it means to create on a dimensional level and how to attain this level. ConvoluTed intends on reminding us, lyrically and vibrationally, what this level feels like.

With an educational background in Biology, Latin languages (French and Spanish), and a multi-instrumental foundation (Guitar, Bass, Drums, and Piano), ConvoluTed is fully equipped and dedicated to sharing his unique perspective and insight on how to connect with the best version of ourselves. His originality and natural music inclination often leaves many wondering how the inspiration came to be and where it came from. Regarding art influences, ConvoluTed is influenced by soulful, developed, intricate, and yet simple, timeless classics of many genres. Regarding energetic influences, ConvoluTed applies and practices the art/awareness of tapping into the infinite stream of creative energy available to all of us by way of going within. Exposure to specific knowledge reveals to us how we are 2 magical entities; our soul and our body. The knowledge of how these 2 facets of ourselves become limitlessly powerful when they are in harmony, reveals the total control of reality we indeed have and how we receive whatever we ask for. ConvoluTed has been put on this earth to share this knowledge via lyrics and demonstrate this power, first hand, by his energetic waves of creation in the form of music.
